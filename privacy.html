Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 15, 2024

Your Privacy Matters

We're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your data. This policy explains our practices in plain language.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our coaching services:

Data Type What We Collect Why We Need It
Account Information Email address, password (encrypted), display name, profile preferences Create and secure your account, personalize your experience
Learning Data Quiz results, tool progress, session notes, practice responses, skill assessments Track your progress, provide personalized coaching, save your work
Session Data Booking details, session recordings (with consent), uploaded materials, coaching notes Deliver coaching services, enable session replay, maintain learning records
Payment Information Billing details processed by Stripe (we don't store card numbers), purchase history Process payments, manage subscriptions, provide customer support
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device info, usage analytics, cookies Ensure platform security, improve performance, analyze usage patterns
Communication Data Support messages, feedback, survey responses Provide customer support, improve our services

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide access to tools, save your progress, deliver coaching sessions
  • Personalization: Customize your learning experience, recommend relevant content
  • Payment Processing: Handle subscriptions, process purchases, manage billing
  • Communication: Send service updates, respond to support requests, share important notices
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, develop new features, enhance user experience
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, protect platform integrity
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements, respond to legal requests

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract Performance: To provide the services you've purchased or subscribed to
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services, ensure security, and communicate with you
  • Consent: For session recordings, marketing communications, and optional features
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing

We don't sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Stripe (payments), Supabase (database), Daily.co/VooV (video calls), Calendly (scheduling)
  • Coaches: Your assigned coaches receive relevant learning data to provide personalized instruction
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale (with notice to users)

Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with trusted third-party services. Each has their own privacy policies: Stripe, Supabase, Daily.co, Calendly.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
Correction
Update or correct inaccurate personal information
Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
Portability
Export your data in a machine-readable format
Restriction
Limit how we process your personal information
Objection
Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, email privacy@chizsaleei.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, compliant cloud platforms
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling potential data breaches

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for different periods based on type and purpose:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account, plus 30 days for recovery
  • Learning Progress: Retained while your account is active to maintain continuity
  • Session Recordings: 1 year after session date (or until you request deletion)
  • Payment Records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Support Communications: 3 years for quality assurance
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 2 years

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, security, and basic functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and customizations

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if disabled.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions for countries with equivalent privacy laws
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we've collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Session Recording and Consent

Coaching sessions may be recorded for educational purposes:

  • Recording requires explicit consent from all participants
  • You can withdraw consent and request deletion at any time
  • Recordings are stored securely and shared only with your assigned coaches
  • Automatic deletion after 1 year unless you request earlier removal

12. Marketing Communications

We may send you:

  • Service Updates: Important notices about your account or services (required)
  • Educational Content: Learning tips, new features, coaching insights (opt-in)
  • Promotional Offers: Special deals and new service announcements (opt-in)

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime using the link provided or by contacting us.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • Prominent notice on our platform
  • In-app notification when you next log in

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • General Support: chizsaleei@gmail.com
  • Website: chizsaleei.com
  • Response Time: Within 30 days for privacy requests, 48 hours for general inquiries
