1. Introduction

CHIZSA COACHING ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our coaching tools, or engage with our services including archetype assessments, profile-based coaching tools, focus area resources, training programs, and the Master Learning Studio.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Access the Master Learning Studio or other free tools

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver personalized coaching tools, assessments, and resources
  • Improve Experience: Enhance our tools and develop new coaching resources
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analytics: Understand how our tools are used to improve effectiveness
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in operating our website and tools

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
