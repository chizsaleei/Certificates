Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are central to how our coaching site and tools are designed.

Last Updated: October 2025

1) Who we are

CHIZSA COACHING (“we”, “us”) provides simple coaching tools and learning resources at chizsaleei.com.

2) What we collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details (e.g., email) when you message us or sign up for forms.
  • Inputs you type into our tools (e.g., quiz answers, notes, goals) when you choose to save them.

Information we automatically receive

  • Basic device and usage data (browser, pages viewed, timestamps, approximate location from IP).
  • Cookies needed for site features and (if enabled) anonymous analytics.

3) How the web tools work

Many tools run fully in your browser. Your entries stay locally in memory unless you click a button to save or share. If you choose to save, we store data in our secure database (see Supabase below). You can request deletion anytime.

4) Supabase integration

What is Supabase? A secure backend platform we use for authentication, database, and file storage.

What we use Supabase for

  • Auth (optional): Account sign-in to sync your tool data across devices.
  • Database: Saving quiz results, notes, and preferences when you opt in.
  • Storage: Optional upload features (e.g., importing a plan) if offered.

How your data is protected

  • Transport encryption (HTTPS) for all traffic.
  • Role-Level Security (RLS) so your records are accessible only to your account.
  • Access limited to operational team members with need-to-know.

What we do not do

  • No sale of personal data.
  • No ad retargeting or third-party ad networks.
  • No reading of your private entries without your request or legal requirement.

5) Cookies & analytics

We use essential cookies for things like keeping you signed in. If we run privacy-respecting analytics, we aggregate or anonymize results. You can set your browser to block non-essential cookies; site features may be limited.

6) How we use information

  • Provide and improve tools you request.
  • Respond to messages and support requests.
  • Understand usage to make the site faster and clearer.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

7) When we share information

  • Service providers: e.g., Supabase, email services—only what’s needed to run the site.
  • Legal: If required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Consent: If you explicitly ask us to share (e.g., with a collaborator).

8) Data retention

Saved tool data is retained while your account is active or as needed for the purpose it was collected. You can request deletion at any time.

9) Your rights

  • Access, correct, export, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features (saving, emails).
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies/analytics.

10) Children

Our services are for ages 18+. We don’t knowingly collect data from children under 18.

11) International transfers

Data may be processed in regions where our providers operate. We use safeguards consistent with this policy.

12) Changes to this policy

We’ll post updates here and refresh the “Last Updated” date. Material changes may be summarized in-app.
